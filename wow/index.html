Flight conditions

Flights are organized all over the world, shuttles are available to go to the take-off site.

On the first day there is an initiation including flight tests, but also a fun part that will please everyone.

At the end of the day the take-off takes place, with everything you desire on board, refreshments, food, personal services with one steward per person.

The different trips offer different itineraries, the proposed route is already an experience out of the ordinary as you will discover the asteroid sea, stops will also be proposed if necessary to set off to space taverns.

The last part of the trip is the arrival at the site, the moon. Once you land, shuttles will allow you to reach your hotels in all tranquility. This whole trip is carried out by MoonPath, your lunar travel agency.

Best-rated hotel

The Lunatic Hotel

Located at the level of the Plato crater, you will have a unique view on the asteroid sea.
The hotel is close to many tourist sites such as the famous Neil Armstrong's footprint.
With its numerous animations and its club The Little Aliens, it will satisfy children and parents.

Curiosities

Lunar Rover 2000

The off-road king

Thanks to our new off-road vehicle, the Moon Rover 2000, our most adventurous tourists can set off to discover the most hostile territories of Earth 1 with our supervisory teams, the Discoverers.
The Moon Rover 2000 is a vehicle with a power of 300-hp and an outstanding agility, moreover with their new gravitation engine you will be able to have fun freely on the lunar regolith or in the air up to 4 meters, all combining extreme sensation and safety.

Neil Armstrong's Footprint

In the footsteps of the man

Discover Neil Armstrong's mythical footprint and the American flag, which are found in the sea of tranquility.
With its famous museum close to the site you can continue your historical experience, this one will please both young and old. Indeed, this one mixes basic and precise knowledge and this in a very playful way.

Dominos Mall

We are wherever you are

The shopping center Dominos is the largest shopping center on the moon, with 100 shops, 2 amusement parks and the largest bubble pool in the universe.
It will delight everyone, buyers but also people who come to discover the center thanks to its many activities of all kinds.
